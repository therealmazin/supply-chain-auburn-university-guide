## What They Make

Fedrigoni specializes in premium materials for:

### Wine Labels
They're the **global leader** in wine label paper. If you've bought wine from Italy, France, or California, there's a good chance the label was made by Fedrigoni.

### Luxury Packaging
High-end boxes for brands like Louis Vuitton, Gucci, and Apple use Fedrigoni materials.

### Art Papers
Papers for artists, designers, and printers who need exceptional quality.

### Self-Adhesive Labels
Labels for food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and more.

## Their Supply Chain Challenge

Like many manufacturers, Fedrigoni deals with:

- **Complex production** - Thousands of paper types, each with different specifications
- **Global customers** - Orders from around the world with different delivery expectations
- **Variable lead times** - Some products can be made quickly, others take weeks
- **Quality requirements** - Premium products mean zero tolerance for defects

## The Converting Process

In paper manufacturing, **"converting"** means transforming raw paper into finished products:

1. **Raw paper** comes from paper mills
2. **Converting machines** cut, coat, print, or laminate the paper
3. **Finished products** ship to customers

The converting step is where most delays happen. That's what we'll analyze.

## Why This Company?

Fedrigoni is a great case study because:

1. **Real business problem** - Late deliveries cost them money
2. **Rich data** - They track detailed order information
3. **Clear metrics** - We can measure success (on-time delivery rate)
4. **Actionable insights** - Predictions can actually change decisions
